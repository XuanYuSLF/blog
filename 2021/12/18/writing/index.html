英语写作分析

作文分析

作文一:

原作文(求助信)，未经修改(考试的时候写得老开心了:( ):

分析(Thanks teacher Zheng):

  1. In English studying, I have problem a lot.

    错误之处:

    1. problem不止一个，应改为problems
    2. have后不能跟双宾语，其中a lot是个名词短语，可替换为loads of表大量许多

    修改后: In English studying,I have loads of problems.

  2. Firstly, there are a lot of strange words,and is difficult to memorize.

    错误之处:

    1. 主谓不一致, 因将”is”改为”are”

    句式升级 :

    1. “and”改为”which”,以构成非限制性定语从句

    修改后: Firstly, there are a lot of strange words,which are difficult to memorize.

  3. And next, in writing section, it is how to use some senior word to design that I don’t know.

    阐明句子结构: 该句为强调句嵌套宾语从句

    错误之处:

    1. design及物动词后没有宾语，应添加”sentences”

    修改后: And next, in writing section, it is how to use some senior word to design sentences that I don’t know.

  4. Improving some advice for you, I hope you could adopt.

    阐明句子结构：该句为现在分词作状语

    错误之处:

    1. 单词记忆错误 “Improving”意为“提升”,所需要为提供”Providing”

    修改后: Providing some advice for you, I hope you could adopt.

  5. First of all, I do hope you introduct some good ways which are good for English studying.

    错误之处:

    1. 单词记忆错误 “introduct”正确拼写应为”introduce”,本人记忆该单词名词形式”introduction”,以导致动词记忆出错

    可改进方面: 该句使用定语从句，先行词为”some good ways”，后关系代词使用”that”更好,若先行词做宾语，最好将关系代词省略

    修改后: First of all, I do hope you introduce some good ways that are good for English studying.

  6. Secondly, you can teach us according to some interesting things,that is because it can improve our curiosities in class.

    错误之处:

    1. teach用法不得当, teach sb. sth. 句式中无sth. 因将”according to”删去
    2. “things,that is …”，本人本想使用“that is because”以构成表语从句，无意中嵌套了非限制定语从句，并且连接词使用错误，以致语法错误

    句式升级: 将interesting改为后置定语, 在”things”后加”making us interested”

    修改后: Secondly, you can teach us some things making us interested,which is because it can improve our curiosities in class.

  7. Last one,it is important for the relationship between students and teacher.

    错误之处 :

    1. 形式主语it并未指代任何主语

    可改为It is important for us to set a good relationship between us

    改进之处:”Last one”改为”Last but not least”

    修改后:Last but not least,it is important for us to set a good relationship between us

  8. 结尾:可改为”I’m looking forward to receiving a letter from you”
